Doggos

Pip

This excitable dog is loving towards her owner, and will always come when called. She is not afraid of strangers, and gets along well with other animals. She's smart and can easily open doors and containers. She is generally clean. She is slightly playful and can entertain herself for hours on end. She's quiet until she's hungry. She will steal food from plates if she is not stopped. Her favorite dog food is cooked chicken. She is street-smart.

Tess

This friendly dog is indifferent toward his owner, and never comes when called. He immediately gets involved with the personal belongings of any new person who enters his space, and does not mind other animals. He's extremely dopey and does not know any basic commands. He is generally extremely clean. He is housebroken. He is extremely playful and can entertain himself for hours on end with any items found on the floor. He likes to talk at his toys.

Rizzo

This gentle dog is aloof toward her owner, and never comes when called. She always acts as though any stranger she meets will harm her, and dislikes other animals. She's smart and very crafty. She is only slightly clean and hates having her teeth cleaned; she rarely cleans herself after toileting. She is not very playful She's only quiet when she's asleep or up to something. She will refuse to move when it's time to end the walk. She loves to roam.
